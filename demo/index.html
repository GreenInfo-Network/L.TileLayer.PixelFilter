Basic functionality

In index.js the L.tileLayerPixelFilter is given RGB codes to fill in the selected areas with red and the un-selected areas with nothing.

setPixelCodes() takes a list of [r,g,b] trios and will highlight pixels whose RGB is on the list.
• A blank list will highlight everything (All Zones).
• A list of impossible codes such as [-1,-1,-1] will match nothing (No Zones).

Masking functionality

In index.js the L.tileLayerPixelFilter is given RGB codes to fill in the selected areas with nothing (alpha=0) and the de-selected areas with white.

Combined with a second layer that is these same tiles with no filtering at all, you can achieve a cool masking effect.

In this case, the mask does cover up the basemap so you may want to skip the basemap in favor of overlaying labels and boundaries over the analytical overlay.
